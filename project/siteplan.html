Site Plan for Portfolio Website

Content

Site Name

BDTPortfolio.com

Site Purpose

To showcase Brylle Dann's work, skills, and services in a professional and visually appealing manner. It serves as a digital resume and a platform for attracting potential clients, employers, or collaborators.

Scenarios

  • Question: How can I see examples of Brylle's previous web design projects?
    Answer: A portfolio section on the website showcases various web design projects with including images, descriptions of the design process, and links to live websites.
  • Question: What services does Brylle offer, and how can I hire him for a project?
    Answer: A services section outlines the different design services offered along with a contact page or form for inquiries. This section also includes information about the process of working with Brylle, pricing details, and testimonials from previous clients.
